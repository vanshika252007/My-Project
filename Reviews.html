Pune Street Food Reviews

Exploring the best bites from Pune’s local food joints

Shawarma

From: Shawarma Joint, FC Road

Review: Juicy and flavorful with perfectly grilled meat, soft pita, and spicy mayo. A great grab-and-go meal.
  • Fresh ingredients
  • Good portion size
  • Sometimes too oily
  • Gets crowded in evenings

Sandwich

From: Ambika Sandwich, Deccan

Review: Classic grilled sandwich with a spicy chutney twist. Crispy outside and soft inside, great street snack.
  • Affordable and tasty
  • Fast service
  • Limited seating

Burger (Camp Burger)

From: Burger King, Pune Camp

Review: Local style burger known for its spicy patty and loaded sauces, messy but delicious.
  • Unique taste, very flavorful
  • Good value for money
  • Not very consistent

Pani Puri

From: Shubham Chaat Center, JM Road

Review: Crispy puris, tangy flavored water, and spicy potato mix, an absolute must try!
  • Freshly made puris
  • Balanced flavors
  • Peak hours can get messy

Bhel

From: Kalyan Bhel, Karve Road

Review: Light, crunchy, and full of flavor. Great mix of sweet, tangy, and spicy, a Pune favorite.
  • Hygienic and flavorful
  • Good for sharing
  • Can get soggy if not eaten quickly
